Our Mission

Our mission is to create an easy of way of learning by providing easy access to study guides, learning materials and interactive learning between learners and teachers. Ulwendo Services is committed to creating a user friendly learning platform for learners and teachers where learners will get guidance on how to effectively plan for the exams and also provide guidance on how to answer exam questions.

FAQs

What does Ulwendo do?

Ulwendo assists learners to have an interactive way of learning with peers and trained teachers.

Who does Ulwendo work with?

Ulwendo works with qualified teachers who mentor learners via this digital learning platform.

Our Vision

Creating a dynamic education digital system that will help learners attain higher level of learning through interacting with other peers and teachers across Zambia.

Corporate Leadership

Lengwe Ng'andwe Chief Executive Officer | Cofounder

Our CEO, Lengwe, Ulwendo Cofounder and business operations growth has been an operating officer galvanizing people and business operations. Lengwe was born on 17.01.1990 and attended primary education at Mufulira Basic and Ipusukilo High School between 1997 and 2008. In 2010, Lengwe went on to the University of Zambia and graduated with Bachelors Degree in Education with Arts. He coined the idea of Ulwendo in the year 2018 after realizing how technology can assist Zambians revise past exam papers.

Kalenga Bwalya Chief Executive Officer | Cofounder | Software Developer

Ulwendo Cofounder and developer, Kalenga is the developer and the driving force for web and mobile development whose focus has been towards improving the platform. Born in 1994, he attended primary education at Roan Primary School in Luanshya between 2000 and 2006. In 2013, Kalenga went to the University of Zambia and graduated with Bachelors Degree in Software Engineering. Together with his fellow CEO, Kalenga has been involved in almost all angles of the initiation, development and deployment processes of Ulwendo services.
